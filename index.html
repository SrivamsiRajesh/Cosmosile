What is Cosmosile Space Club
Cosmosile Space Club is a community for space enthusiasts of all ages. We offer events, workshops, and discussions that inspire curiosity and foster a deeper understanding of the universe.
Are there any age restrictions for joining the local space club
Most of our events are family-friendly and open to all ages. However, some specialized workshops may have specific age recommendations. Please refer to individual event descriptions for more information.
